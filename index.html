Research Interests

I am interested in machine learning and automated theorem proving. I enjoy exploring how these two fields can mutually benefit each-other. I believe that machine learning can benefit automated reasoning by providing heuristics and I believe that automated reasoning can benefit machine learning by providing strong inductive biases.
